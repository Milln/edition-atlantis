      lassen, die allzu oft verkannt, missverstanden und verunglimpft wurden.

  en:
    text: >-
      This book has no other ambition than to help restore some flouted truths
      and to fight against the misinformation that has surrounded the history of
      French Algeria for more than 50 years. It is particularly intended for the
      young generations of French families who had to leave Algeria at the time
      of its independence in 1962. Its presentation in the form of questions and
      answers makes it easier and more practical to read.


      However, this is not a children's book, as certain passages, and in
      particular some accounts reported by witnesses of dramatic events, could
      offend their sensitivity.


      It will, however, be able to help their parents answer the main questions
      that might be asked of them and thus contribute to doing justice to a
      community and a page of history that are too often misunderstood,
      misinterpreted, and criticized.


      Finally, this book will constitute a synthetic approach for anyone wishing
      to be informed about what the work of France in Algeria really was.


      New edition, revised and expanded from the 2002 edition.
