      Und jetzt taucht alles wieder vor mir auf: der Krieg, der Verrat, die

      Ab Oktober 1960 organisierte der Unteroffizier der Luftwaffe Henri
      Mazzarino den Widerstand in Blida. Einige Monate später schloss sich Mazza
      mit seiner Gruppe der OAS an. Im Oktober 1961 desertierte er und ging in
      den Untergrund. Blida war die "einzige Stadt, in der die OAS einen
      repräsentativen Charakter annahm" (Pierre Montagnon, La Guerre d’Algérie).


      In diesem Buch beschreibt er seinen Weg zwischen 1958 und 1964, einen Weg,
      der sehr gefährlich, manchmal abenteuerlich und oft bewegend war.

      Through my story, it is the story of many of my fellow combatants, both
      civilian and military, who in the shadows tried to save French Algeria.


      Some lost their lives, others went to prison, and others, lucky ones, made
      it through everything.


      It is to all of them that I wish to pay tribute.


      "Until now, I have never managed to tell this story to the end, because
      the emotion catches in my throat.


      And now everything rises up before me again: the war, the betrayals, the
      permanent insecurity, the risk of being kidnapped, of being arrested, and
      all this atmosphere of general disarray, piles of trash burning on the
      sidewalks, a city that is dying, planes and boats arriving in trickles,
      friends who died, those who were kidnapped... How can one forgive?..."


      Starting in October 1960, Air Force non-commissioned officer Henri
      Mazzarino organized the resistance in Blida. A few months later, Mazza
      joined the OAS with his group. In October 1961, he deserted and went
      underground. Blida would be the "only city where the OAS took on a
      representative character" (Pierre Montagnon, La Guerre d’Algérie).


      In this book, he describes his journey between 1958 and 1964, a journey
      that was very dangerous, sometimes dramatic, and often moving.
