      Nach dem schmerzhaften Exodus von 1962 richtet Jean Brune einen bewegenden
      Appell zur Versöhnung an seine Pied-Noir-Landsleute.


      Wenn alles zusammenbricht, haben formale Werte keine Gültigkeit mehr. Man

      als Wegweiser wiederfindet, die es in Afrika verschmäht hat.


      Der Traum beginnt von neuem. (Jean Brune, 1963)

  en:
    text: >-
      Following the painful exodus of 1962, Jean Brune launches a moving appeal
      for reconciliation to his fellow Pied-Noir countrymen.


      When everything collapses, formal values no longer hold sway. One must
      reinvent duty and not weave derisory intrigues.


      Establish yourself proudly and await the future, it will be born naturally
      from your silences.

       The future is merely fervor to be put in order. This void you discover around you is the opportunity that destiny offers you. Here is a France to be made. You were a seed in Africa, you will be a seed in Europe.

      In the general transformation of the world, what you do with the French
      can reach such a dimension of example that France will rediscover this
      role of pioneer that it disdained in Africa.


      The dream begins again. (Jean Brune, 1963)
