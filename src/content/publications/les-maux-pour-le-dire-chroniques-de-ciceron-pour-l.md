      Under the pseudonym of the great rhetorician Cicero, Maurice Calmein
      published the column "Les maux pour le dire" in the Voix du Midi-Lauragais
      for two years. In his incisive and humorous entries, he attacks the
      current drifts of the French language, the invasion of Anglicisms, and a
      certain pedantry of language that is detrimental to simple and effective
      communication. The success of this column with the newspaper's readers
      gave rise to the idea of gathering 90 of these entries in the present
      collection.
