    text: >-
      I wrote this memoir for two very simple reasons.


      The first was to satisfy the need to relive, in a sense, the years of my
      childhood and adolescence in a country that I loved very much. After years
      of moving across the world due to my husband's professional requirements,
      I felt the need to find my roots.


      The second reason is the desire to pass on a legacy: to share the lives of
      the French in Algeria during the period of French presence through the
      people I knew, including friends and family members. It is also to evoke
      the years of war until our final departure.


      Featuring 77 watercolors, sketches, and photos by the author.


      Large format 30 x 20 cm.

      82 pages, including 36 in color.
