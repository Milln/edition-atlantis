      They are part of the caracoles (snails), a nickname given to Spanish

      "The Spanish arrived in compact hordes in the province of Oran,
      accompanied by their wives and a swarming progeny.


      They went off like this, sun-tanned and ragged, weighed down by the burden
      of poverty, towards the unknown, in search of a crust of bread."
      (Jean-Jacques Jordi, Espagnol en Oranie)
